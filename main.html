A car is a four-wheeled motor vehicle used for transportation. It typically runs on gasoline or diesel. The engine converts fuel into mechanical power to move the car. The transmission controls the speed and direction. Cars come in various sizes and types to suit different needs. They have seats for passengers and storage space for goods. Wheels and tires provide mobility and traction on the road. Modern cars often come with advanced features like air conditioning and infotainment systems. Safety features like airbags and ABS are standard in most cars. Cars have become an integral part of modern life, providing convenience and independence for individuals and families.
